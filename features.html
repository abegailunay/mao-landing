Dashboard

The Dashboard is your real-time command center. It gives HR owners and admins an at-a-glance view of today's workforce status, pending approvals, and payroll health — all in one place.

Shows every active employee and their current status for today:

  • Clocked In — employee has clocked in on time
  • Late — clocked in after the grace period; shows scheduled shift time and actual clock-in time
  • On Break — currently on a logged break
  • Not In — has not clocked in yet for today

You can filter by branch using the All Sites / Main toggle at the top right of the table.

A consolidated list of all items waiting for your action, pulled from across the platform:

  • Leave Requests — employees requesting time off
  • Overtime Exceptions — detected early or late clock-ins/outs needing approval
  • Correction Requests — employees requesting edits to their attendance logs
  • Payroll Disputes — submitted disputes on released payslips

Clicking any item takes you directly to the relevant approval page.

Shows the most recently released payroll run for your business — including gross pay, net pay, headcount, and the cutoff period it covers. Useful for a quick sanity check after each payroll release.

The Financials card gives Tenant Owners a financial snapshot of the current month:

  • Payroll Released This Month — total net payroll paid out across all released runs in the current calendar month
  • vs. last cycle — percentage change compared to the previous payroll cycle, so you can spot unusual spikes or drops
  • Overtime (Latest Run) — total overtime pay from the most recent payroll run

This card is only visible to users with the Tenant Owner role.

Forces a fresh reload of all dashboard data from the server, bypassing the local cache. Use this if you've just made changes elsewhere (e.g. approved a leave request, edited an attendance log) and want the dashboard to reflect them immediately. The dashboard also auto-refreshes every 60 seconds in the background.

Generates a printable PDF snapshot of the current day's Attendance Health table. Useful for end-of-day records, supervisor reports, or offline filing. The export captures all employees and their status at the moment you click the button.

Employees have their own separate dashboard in the Employee Self-Service (ESS) portal, which shows their personal attendance, schedule, payslips, and leave balances.

Payroll

The Payroll module handles the full payroll cycle — from setting up cutoff periods to generating drafts, approving runs, releasing payslips, and managing disputes.

Payroll cutoffs define the work period covered by each payroll run. You can set these up manually or use a cutoff template to generate them automatically (e.g. 1st–15th and 16th–end of month for semi-monthly payroll).

  • Open — active cutoff, attendance is still being recorded
  • Closed — period is locked, payroll run can be generated

A payroll run is created against a cutoff period. The lifecycle of a run is:

  • Draft — generated from attendance data; review employee computations
  • Approved — HR owner has signed off on the draft
  • Released — payslips are visible to employees in ESS

You can generate a draft, refresh it after making corrections, approve it, and release it. Each step requires deliberate action — nothing releases automatically.

After payslips are released, employees can file disputes if they believe their pay was computed incorrectly. Disputes appear here for HR to review, request more information, or resolve. Each dispute has a messaging thread so HR and the employee can communicate directly.

Add one-time or recurring deductions for individual employees — cash advances, equipment loans, uniform fees, etc. Deductions are automatically applied to the next applicable payroll run and carried forward if not fully deducted.

HR Records

HR Records covers leave management, memos, and incident reports — the documentation side of managing your team.

View and action all leave requests from employees. You can approve, reject, or request more information. Approved leaves are automatically reflected in payroll computations for the relevant cutoff period.

Issue formal memos to employees — written warnings, notices to explain, show-cause letters, and other disciplinary communications. Each memo has:

  • A severity level (e.g. Verbal Warning, Written Warning, Final Warning)
  • Nature of violation, consequences, and employer expectation fields
  • An optional file attachment
  • An expiration date (auto-calculated from severity settings)
  • An escalation path — if a new incident occurs, you can escalate to a higher severity memo linked to the original

Employees receive a notification and must acknowledge the memo in their ESS portal.

Employees can file incident reports directly from the ESS portal. HR can review, update the status, and use incident reports as supporting documentation when issuing memos.

Attendance

The Attendance module gives you full visibility into employee clock-in/out records, daily summaries, exceptions, and correction requests.

The raw clock-in and clock-out records for every employee. You can filter by date, employee, branch, status, or cutoff period. HR can also add manual entries, edit existing logs, or delete records when needed. Each log shows clock-in photo if photo verification is enabled.

Computed daily attendance summaries per employee — showing total payable hours, late minutes, overtime hours, and attendance status (Present, Late, Absent, Half-day). These summaries feed directly into payroll draft generation.

Attendance exceptions are automatically flagged when an employee clocks in early, late, or outside their scheduled shift. Exceptions require HR approval before they affect payroll. You can approve or reject exceptions individually or in bulk.

Employees can submit correction requests from ESS when they believe their clock-in or clock-out time was wrong (e.g. forgot to clock out, wrong time recorded). HR reviews and approves or rejects these requests. Approved corrections update the attendance log automatically.

When enabled, MAO restricts clock-ins to employees within a defined radius of your workplace. Set up your geofence under Settings → Attendance by dropping a pin on the map and setting the allowed radius. Employees outside the radius will be blocked from clocking in via the ESS portal.

Scheduling

Set up shift templates, assign schedules to employees, manage your weekly roster, and handle shift swaps and temporary changes.

Define reusable shift configurations — name, start time, end time, unpaid break duration, and grace period. Templates are assigned to employees and used as the basis for attendance exception detection and payroll computation.

Assign shift templates to specific employees for specific date ranges. An employee can have different shifts on different days. Schedule assignments must be approved before they take effect.

The Weekly Roster gives you a 7-day grid view of all employee schedules — useful for spotting gaps or conflicts. Daily Staffing shows who is scheduled for a specific date, by branch and role.

Employees can request to swap shifts with a colleague directly from ESS. The swap goes through an approval flow: the target employee accepts, then HR approves. Approved swaps update both employees' schedules automatically.

Workforce

Manage your employee roster, company directory, and organizational structure — branches, roles, and departments.

The full list of all employees in your business. From here you can add new employees, view and edit employee profiles, manage compensation, assign roles, set up kiosk PIN access, and provision ESS login accounts.

Each employee profile has tabs for: Profile, Employment Details, Compensation, Roles, Schedule, Kiosk Access, and Memos.

A searchable directory of all active employees — with profile photos, roles, departments, and contact info. Employees can also view and update their own directory profile from ESS.

Define the organizational structure of your business. Branches represent physical locations or cost centers. Roles define job positions (e.g. Cashier, Team Lead, Cook) and can have associated daily rate overrides. Departments group roles together for reporting purposes.

Settings

Configure your business — payroll rules, attendance policies, statutory rates, holidays, memo types, and leave types.

Configure overtime multipliers (regular OT, rest day OT, holiday OT), night differential rules, and whether overtime requires prior approval. These settings apply globally to all payroll runs for your business.

Configure photo verification requirements for clock-in and clock-out, and set up geofencing. Photo modes: Disabled, Required for Clock-In, Required for Clock-Out, or Required for Both.

MAO comes pre-loaded with the current SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG contribution tables. You can view the active tables and update them when the government releases new rates. These are used automatically in every payroll run.

Manage the holiday calendar for your business. MAO can import Philippine public holidays automatically by year. You can also add custom holidays (e.g. local holidays, company anniversaries). Holiday types and pay multipliers follow DOLE guidelines by default.

Define the memo types (e.g. Attendance, Conduct, Performance) and severity levels (e.g. Verbal Warning, Written Warning, Final Warning) used in your business. Each severity can have a default expiration period in days.

Configure the leave types available to your employees — Sick Leave, Vacation Leave, Emergency Leave, etc. Set accrual rules, whether the leave is paid or unpaid, and whether it requires documentation.

Employee Self-Service (ESS)

ESS is the employee-facing portal where your team members can clock in and out, view their payslips, file leave requests, and more — without needing to contact HR for everything.

Each employee's personal home screen showing their clock-in/out button, today's shift, current attendance status, and leave balance summary. Employees clock in and out directly from this page using their device camera (if photo verification is enabled).

Employees can view all their released payslips, see a full breakdown of earnings and deductions, and download a PDF copy. If they believe a payslip is incorrect, they can file a dispute directly from this page.

A personal log of all the employee's clock-in and clock-out records. Employees can submit correction requests from here if they notice a discrepancy in their attendance data.

Employees can view their leave balances, submit new leave requests, and track the status of pending requests. Approved leave is automatically reflected in their payroll for the relevant period.

Employees can view memos issued to them by HR, submit their written explanation, and formally acknowledge receipt. Unacknowledged memos show a banner reminder on their dashboard until actioned.

A calendar view of the employee's assigned shifts. Employees can also request shift swaps with colleagues from this page.

HR provisions an ESS login from the employee's profile page (Workforce → Employees → [Employee] → Create Login). The employee receives an email invitation to set their password, then logs in at portal.maoworkforce.com.
