Getting Started

MAO Workforce is a Filipino HR and payroll platform built for small and medium businesses. It handles employee scheduling, attendance tracking, payroll computation (including SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and withholding tax), and government compliance reports — all in one place.
Go to portal.maoworkforce.com and click Create Account. Enter your business name, email, and password. You'll start on the Free plan — no credit card required.
Go to Workforce → Employees and click + Add Employee. Fill in their name, employee code, branch, role, and salary details. You can also invite them via email so they can access the Employee Self-Service portal.
Yes. You can manually add your existing employees and their salary details. Historical payroll records can be uploaded as needed. If you need help migrating a large team, email us at hello@maoworkforce.com and we'll assist.
The MAO portal is fully mobile-responsive and works on any browser. We also offer MAO Kiosk — a dedicated tablet/device app for employees to clock in and out without needing a personal device.

Payroll

MAO supports weekly, semi-monthly, and monthly payroll cutoffs. Each payroll cutoff can be customized with its own start and end dates.
MAO pulls each employee's attendance logs for the cutoff period and computes:
  • Regular hours, overtime (OT), night differential (ND)
  • Late deductions and undertime
  • Holiday pay (regular and special)
  • Allowances (monthly or semi-monthly)
  • Statutory deductions: SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and withholding tax
  • Manual deductions (loans, cash advances)
The result is a payslip for each employee that you can review before releasing.
Yes. After generating a payroll draft, you can review each employee's payslip, make manual adjustments, and re-generate before approving and releasing.
Allowances are set per employee under their salary record. You can choose:
  • Monthly — paid once a month on a scheduled day
  • Semi-monthly — the total amount is split in half and paid twice a month (e.g. on the 15th and last day)
Allowances can be taxable or non-taxable.
Yes. MAO supports special payroll runs (SPD) for 13th month pay, bonuses, and other one-time payouts, separate from your regular payroll cycle.

Attendance

Employees can clock in via:
  • MAO Kiosk — a shared tablet at your location, using employee code, PIN, or QR code
  • Employee Self-Service portal — from any browser on their phone or computer
The attendance log stays Open until a clock-out is recorded. Employees can submit a Correction Request for a missed clock-out, which an HR admin can approve. Once approved, the log is updated and payroll hours are recomputed.
Yes. HR admins and branch managers can add manual attendance entries, edit existing logs, or delete records from the Attendance → Logs page.
MAO detects pre-shift and post-shift overtime automatically based on the employee's assigned schedule. Detected overtime appears in Attendance → Exceptions and must be approved by an admin before it's counted in payroll.
Night differential covers hours worked between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM, which are entitled to an additional 10% pay under Philippine labor law. MAO computes ND hours automatically from clock-in/out records.

Compliance (BIR / SSS / PhilHealth / Pag-IBIG)

Yes. MAO uses the current contribution tables for SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG and automatically computes both employee and employer shares during payroll generation. Contribution tables are updated when government rates change.
Yes. MAO computes monthly withholding tax using the BIR tax table based on each employee's gross taxable income. Note: MAO provides computation assistance — you remain responsible for filing and remittance to BIR directly.
Yes. MAO generates statutory reports showing SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and withholding tax summaries per payroll period, which you can use to prepare your remittance forms.
Yes. You can run a special payroll for 13th month pay. MAO computes the amount based on each employee's basic salary and months of service within the calendar year.

Managing Employees

Yes. Once invited, employees can log in to the MAO Employee Self-Service portal to view their payslips, attendance records, leave balances, and submit requests.
Go to Workforce → Employees, open the employee's profile, and click Invite to Portal. They'll receive an email with a link to set up their account.
Yes. MAO supports multiple branches under one business account. Employees are assigned to a branch, and you can filter attendance, payroll, and reports by branch.
MAO has these access levels:
  • Tenant Owner — full access to everything
  • HR Admin — manages employees, attendance, and payroll
  • Payroll Admin — manages payroll runs and reports
  • Branch Manager — manages their branch's attendance and scheduling
  • Employee — self-service access only

Security & Data

Yes. MAO runs on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security — each business's data is strictly isolated. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.
